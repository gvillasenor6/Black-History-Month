Christina Morillo


Christina Morillo is a technology leader and advocate for diversity in tech, known for her work in cybersecurity and cloud infrastructure, and for co-founding Women of Color in Tech Chat, aimed at providing visibility and resources for women of color in the industry. Additionally, she has worked with companies across the tech and financial sectors.She is a published author, speaker, and guest editor at O'Reilly Media. Christina is now pursuing a C.S. graduate degree at Georgia Tech while serving as a Fellow and Advisor for the #ShareTheMicInCyber project and Fellowship at New America. Christina is dedicated to making information security more approachable by demystifying technology. Her overarching goal is to keep people, organizations, and communities safe.
